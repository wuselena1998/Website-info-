Welcome to the Radiation Awareness Website

What is Radiation?

Radiation is energy that comes from a source and travels through space. It can be natural (like sunlight) or artificial (like X-rays and nuclear power).

Types of Radiation

  • Alpha particles
  • Beta particles
  • Gamma rays
  • Neutrons

Safety and Protection

There are many ways to protect yourself from harmful radiation, including shielding, distance, and limiting exposure time.
