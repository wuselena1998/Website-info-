Radiation in Cities

New York City

New York has low natural radiation levels. Most radiation exposure comes from medical sources like X-rays, CT scans, and cancer treatments in hospitals across the city.

Tokyo, Japan

Following the Fukushima nuclear incident in 2011, Tokyo saw minor radiation effects, but today it remains a safe city with very low background radiation levels, monitored carefully by the government.

Chernobyl, Ukraine

Chernobyl is the site of the most serious nuclear accident in history (1986). The surrounding exclusion zone still has high radiation levels, although some areas have been opened for short visits with proper safety precautions.
