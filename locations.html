Radiation Levels in New York, Tokyo, and Chernobyl

New York

New York has natural background radiation from cosmic rays, radon gas in older buildings, and medical procedures like X-rays and CT scans.

Tokyo

Tokyo had radiation concerns after the 2011 Fukushima accident. While levels rose temporarily, today Tokyo’s background radiation is very low and safe.

Chernobyl

Chernobyl is the site of the 1986 nuclear disaster. Even today, radiation levels in the Exclusion Zone are dangerous, although certain areas have become surprisingly resilient with wildlife.
